Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 02, 2026

1. Data Collection and Privacy

We do not collect, store, or share any of your personal information. All data entered into the UAJK CGPA Calculator, including your name, department, and academic results, is stored securely and entirely locally on your device. We do not have access to your data, and it is never transmitted to any external servers.

2. App Scope & Disclaimer

This application is designed specifically according to the Higher Education Commission (HEC) criteria for the University of Azad Jammu and Kashmir (UAJK). Please note:

  • The calculation logic is based on current HEC/UAJK standards. If these criteria change, we will endeavor to update the app accordingly.
  • Currently, only the Department of Computer Science is covered. Support for other departments will be added in future updates.
  • This application is an independent project and may eventually be handed over to the Faculty of the University for official management.

3. Third-Party Services (Advertising)

While the app itself does not collect personal data, we use third-party services like Google AdMob to display advertisements. These third-party services may collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your device IP address and advertising ID, to provide relevant ads and analyze ad performance.

You can learn more about how Google handles data in its advertising products by visiting the Google Ads Privacy Policy.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Any updates will be reflected directly in this document.

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support.meetops@gmail.com.
